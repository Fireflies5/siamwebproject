SOCIETY FOR INDUSTRIAL AND APPLIED MATHEMATICS


SIAM stands for Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics. It is one of the most prestigious community where enthusiasts of mathematics and its applied applications can contribute, nurture and enhance their interests. Applied mathematics, in partnership with computational science is essential in solving many real world problems. Our mission is to build cooperation between mathematics and the world of science and technology through publications, research and community through membership activities, publications of journals, books and conferences. For further details, you can go to SIAM website.
SIAM-VIT aims at dispensing the required tools and knowledge for students to excel in fields of applied mathematics such as computational science, forensics, cryptography etc. It provides a bridge for students to apply the classroom knowledge to real world applications and push the boundaries of their curriculum to explore their field of interest through projects, research, industrial visits and internships. It is the only chapter in VIT that takes all the branches under its wings and therefore has a huge outreach.

Our Events

  1. Riveria

    2. VGT: VIT'S Got Talent is one of the biggest hits of Riviera, the cultural fest of VIT Vellore. Here, the participants get to showcase their talent in front of an audience and judges. The best of which are awarded with cash prizes.




  2. Hackulus

    3. Hackulus is a 24hr hackathon organised by Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (SIAM VIT). The event would have multiple tracks and expects to see participants from all parts of the country. It aims to spotlight unique and innovative ideas to real world problems within 24hrs of constant mentorship and guidance by experts in leading technical domains such as Web development, App development and Machine Learning. It also aims to provide an opportunity to beginners who are not familiar with hackathons by providing a separate beginner round.




  3. Gravitas

    4. Gravitas is an international fest organised by VIT, Vellore usually in the month of September/October. The technology oriented fest which continues for three days, provides the students a platform to showcase their talents. Students from different colleges come here to take part in the competitions organised by various chapters.




  4. Incore Sessions

    5. SIAM-VIT believes in fulfilling the conjectures of its core committee and we believe in the betterment and polishing of our core who are the backbone of this chapter. On taking regular feedbacks from our core, we found out their urge to learn fundamentals of photoshop and video editing. Hence it was decided to conduct the incore sessions by our Design and Tech head. A great amount of turnout was seen with huge enthusiasm.
